The Gospel of Thomas

Greek: Παν ἐἄνδρως υμέν εἰπεν ἔνα ζητεῖν καὶ λεγούσαι αἔτι θαῖσας.

Translation: These are the secret sayings that the living Jesus spoke and Didymos Judas Thomas recorded.

Enuma Elish

Akkadian (transliterated): Enuma elish la nabu shamamu, shaplish ammatum shuma la zakrat.

Translation: When on high the heavens had not been named, and below the earth had not been called by name...
